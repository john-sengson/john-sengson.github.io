  • Based on the above description, a commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include - an explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives.

    • I very much have commited myself to following a set routine. I have read up on the student-prep and it gave me a lot of ideas. I have started writing journals to set goals and reflect daily. I use time apps such as toggl and try to stick to time boxes or my own time goals. In this way I dont feel burnt out and I get to see my progress. Having frequent breaks away from the screen (pomodoro break) helps. Having that time away from screen shows that I can break down tasks and manage my time accordigly.

  • A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way.

The time will vary of course base on where I stand in terms of my progress in my current task. If by the set time expired and I can't find any solution via resources I have (google, own notes, cheat sheets), then I will seek the help of my facilitator or cohorts. Slack is good for this as there are different channels I can try and get asssitance from.

Even though I have lived for 35 years and think that I know myself, I expect to complete my journey with EDA with a better understanding and be an improved version of myself, tech and personality wise.
